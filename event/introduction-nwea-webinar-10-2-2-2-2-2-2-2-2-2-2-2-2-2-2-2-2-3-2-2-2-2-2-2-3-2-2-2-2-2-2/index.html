Introduction to NWEA and MAP Webinar

July 19, 2016 @ 1:00 pm - 1:30 pm

  • « Introduction to NWEA and MAP Webinar
  • Introduction to NWEA and MAP Webinar »

Join us for a complimentary webinar that introduces you to the computer adaptive assessment used by thousands of districts across the U.S. to measure student growth and school performance: Measures of Academic Progress® (MAP®).

MAP interim assessments adapt to each student’s learning level. That built-in flexibility means they empower every teacher to personalize instruction, every school to maximize student growth and achievement, and every district to lead change in their community.

Intended Audience: Administrative personnel from schools and districts not currently partnered with NWEA.
